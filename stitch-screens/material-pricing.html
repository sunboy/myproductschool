Common Questions

Can I change my plan later? expand_more
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. If you upgrade, the pro features are unlocked immediately.
What kind of sensors do you support? expand_more
Terra Oak plan supports all major LoRaWAN and WiFi-enabled soil sensors, including brands like GrowPoint, Meter Group, and custom Arduino/Raspberry Pi setups via our API.
Is my data shared with third parties? expand_more
Your ecology data is private to you. We do not sell data. Aggregated, anonymized data may be used to improve our regional growth models, but only if you opt-in to the Open Earth initiative.
Do you offer educational discounts? expand_more
We offer a 50% discount for registered students, schools, and non-profit community gardens. Please contact our support team with your credentials to apply.
