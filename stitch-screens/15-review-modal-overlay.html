The Scenario

Systemic Retention Failure

Following the launch of the v4.2 mobile application, the Product Team observed a significant anomaly in user engagement patterns. While weekday retention remained stable at 68%, there was a sharp, unexplained decline starting every Friday at 18:00 UTC.

Your task was to investigate the metrics_daily.sql payload and identify if the dropoff was localized to specific regions or if it was a latency issue related to the new caching layer implemented for the 'Explore' tab.
