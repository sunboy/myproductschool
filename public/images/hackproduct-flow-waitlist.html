F

01 of 04

Frame.

Before you solve anything, make sure you're solving the right thing. A well-framed problem is already half-answered.

Watch it in action

The prompt says "improve engagement for a music app." A weak answer starts building features. A FLOW answer asks: engagement for whom? Measured how? On what surface? That reframe changes everything.

L

02 of 04

List.

Map the full landscape. Who's affected, what solutions exist, what constraints matter. Breadth now, depth later.

Watch it in action

Users: casual listeners, power curators, artists, advertisers. Solutions span onboarding, social features, algorithmic discovery, offline mode, creator tools. You can't optimize what you haven't enumerated.

O

03 of 04

Optimize.

Now narrow. Weigh trade-offs. Compare impact against effort. Stress-test your own assumptions before someone else does.

Watch it in action

Social features drive retention but take 3 months to build. Algorithmic discovery improves engagement 20% faster with half the engineering cost. The trade-off is clear — but only if you lay it out.

W

04 of 04

Win.

Make the call. State your recommendation, define success metrics, and outline what ships first. Conviction backed by structure.

Watch it in action

Recommendation: Launch personalized "discovery sessions" for casual listeners. Success: +15% weekly active listens within 90 days. V1 scope: curated playlists based on listening gaps, measured by session depth and return rate.
