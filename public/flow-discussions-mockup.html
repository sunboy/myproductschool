Notification Fatigue: Fixing a B2B SaaS Drop-off

Notification Fatigue

Senior PM, Growth B2B SaaS Medium

The situation

You work on a B2B SaaS project management tool. DAU/MAU is declining. Exit surveys cite "too many notifications." The activity digest feature shipped 6 months ago and usage is at 18%.

What just happened

Three enterprise accounts threatened to churn due to "notification overload." Support tickets about notification settings are up 40% MoM.

Your challenge

How do you diagnose and fix the notification fatigue problem without sacrificing engagement for power users?

What metric should you use as the primary success indicator for the fix?

Luma's Team Expert Pick · 2d ago

Notification-open rate is a vanity metric here — it stays high even if users are annoyed. The real signal is weekly active notification interactions per user, segmented by tier. Enterprise churn is the lagging indicator you're trying to move.

AK
alex_k · 1d ago

I used notification dismissal rate as my primary — if users are dismissing without reading, that's the clearest churn signal. Luma's point on segmentation is key though.
